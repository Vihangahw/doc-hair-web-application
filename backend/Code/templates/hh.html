CONGRATULATIONS! YOU HAVE HEALTHY HAIR

Healthy Hair

"Healthy hair is hair that is free of breakage and split ends and is achieving its maximum potential for your particular hair type,” says Lamb.

Hair serves a role in your health — beyond giving you a confidence-boost.
Hair helps regulate your temperature, keeping you warm when it’s cold, and helping wick sweat when you’re hot.
It also helps provide a little padding if you fall, and it may protect your scalp from the sun.

According to the experts, these are some of the main signs of healthy hair, though it may not apply to all hair types.

  • Exhibits little breakage and minimal shedding
  • Does not break when it’s brushed
  • Does not contain dandruff
  • Reflects light
  • Has sheen and luster
  • Contains moisture
