Scalp Psoriasis

Scalp psoriasis is a common skin disorder. It may look different on different skin tones. It can be a single patch or several, and can even affect your entire scalp.
It can also spread to your forehead, the back of your neck, or behind and inside your ears.

Symptoms of Scalp Psoriasis

Symptoms of mild scalp psoriasis may include only slight, fine scaling.
It includes:

  1. Scaly, red or purple bumpy patches
  2. Silvery-white or gray scales
  3. Dandruff-like flaking
  4. Dry scalp
  5. Itching
  6. Hair loss
